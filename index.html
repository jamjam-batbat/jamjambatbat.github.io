FEATURES

🛜 WIReD & WIreless SuPPoRT

As with most headphones in the modern technology industry, the CC-522 Compact Control Headphones™ support wireless connections via Bluetooth, making its connections compatible to devices such as laptops, smartphones, & tablets without the hassle of using wires for your headphones, earphones, or earbuds. However, if your prefer wired headphones over wireless ones, you also have the choice of using your CC-522 Headphones with the AUX wire that came with it.

🔋 LonG PlAyTIme & BATTeRy LIfe

Since it's a pair of Bluetooth headphones, the CC-522 Headphones are also rechargable devices that need to charge after 24 hours of playtime. When not in use, the charged battery lasts for 60 days on standby.

🎧 oPTImIzeD noIse-CAnceLLInG sTeReo

The CC-522 Headphones provides the optimized support for both mono audio & the three-dimensional surround sound of stereo, with the sound system taking up a very small amount of space to make room for other features. However, there are also settings to enable & disable external noise cancellation. Both of these features can be switched around by pressing their respective buttons on the initial left or non-dominant side of the headphones.

👐 AmBIDexTRous AccEssIBILITy

Unlike most headphones in the market today, there is support for both right-handed & left-handed users of the CC-522 model. While the headphones are set in Right-Handed Mode by default, enabling Left-Handed Mode is as easy as flicking a switch on the headphones' non-dominant side, swapping the left & right speakers around to the fit the current mode. This is especially useful for the following feature.

eAsy & usefuL ConTRoLs

The CC-522 Headphones have all the controls that are present in Bluetooth headphones, but they're not just presented as small buttons on each of the two speakers. For this model, TechNova developed a unique controller called the ExceRadius™, which is a control knob & a button at its center on the initial right or dominant side of your earphones. The functions controlled by the ExceRadius™ include, but are not limited to:

  • Increasing & decreasing the device's volume by turning the knob
  • Playing the next video or song by tapping the appropriate space adjacent to the button
  • Fast-forwarding & backtracking to a point in a video or song by double-tapping the appropriate space adjacent to the button
  • Pausing & playing the video or song by pressing the button
  • Answering an incoming call by holding the button

🎤 ReTRAcTABLe MIcRoPHone

The remaining space on the CC-522 model's non-dominant side is used for storing its retractable microphone, which is reminiscent of the microphones that are usually attached to headphones. It's stored in a compartment on that side's speaker, which can be twisted open to reveal & retrieve the microphone to use.

For easier space-saving in your preferred storage, the CC-522 Compact Control Headphones™ have hinges built into them, allowing them to fold & take up much less space. It also has an adjustable base for a comfortable fit on the user's head. This is illustrated in the following preview pictures.
